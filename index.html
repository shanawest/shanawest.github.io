About Shana

Shana has worked in human-centered design since 2008. She joined The Nerdery as a User Experience Designer in 2015. Her work there has focused on business discovery, user research, experience strategy, product roadmapping, service design, information architecture, and interaction design for web, mobile, and desktop applications. Her favorite projects challenge her to make sense of sticky, complex problem spaces and processes.

Shana is excited to work on projects helping people work efficiently, make decisions, and change habits, and seeks to provoke curiosity, conversation, and collaboration through digital experiences. She got her start in human-centered design as an interactive exhibit developer, telling stories about science and history with an emphasis on people and the environment.
